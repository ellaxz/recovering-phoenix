# Recovering Phoenix
### Monash University - 2021S2FIT5120IE - TP28

Our website is a  science and mental health-related information for women aged 16-24, including but not limited to eating disorder, anxiety, depression, and other information.

## About project

Women are involved in all parts of society and lots of female problems keep becoming the hot-points in the around world. Mental health is one of the most important parts. Due to various social factors, such as gender discrimination, sexual abuse, and even some physical injuries, many women are under great pressure, but they choose to remain silent. Some reasons are that they don't know how to seek help, and another reason is that they try to ignore or even deny bad experiences.

The goal of our website is to establish an anonymous female community. They can find some scientific treatments to relieve pain and negative emotions. Or they can share or listen to other people's experience in order to gain more positive power.
